Where are the toughest places for
girls to get an education?


A closer look

  • The worst areas for girl's education are those among the poorest in the world and most poverty-stricken.

  • The toughest countries are mainly concentrated in Africa, with nine of the worst ten located in sub-Saharan Africa.

  • Countries in conflict zones and war-torn areas tend to have worse girl's education.



Data courtesy of the ONE campaign (2017)

#1 SOUTH SUDAN

Girls in South Sudan are least likely to go to school. A young girl in South Sudan is 3 times more likely to die in pregnancy or childbirth than to complete her primary education.
